About Us

CAMAA MULTI-MEDIA NIG LTD is a company founded at the beginning of the millennium by a team of enthusiastic Journalists and IT specialists who wanted to overcome the routine and create a company that would act in the market not only for business and government success but for the sake of Technology itself.

Thus, the mission of the company was defined – to contribute to forward-looking transformation of the society through the use of information communication technology (ICT) journalistic propaganda, and Television content productions.
